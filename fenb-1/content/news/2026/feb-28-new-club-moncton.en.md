---

Fencing New Brunswick is thrilled to welcome the **Dieppe Fencing Academy** as the province's newest affiliated club.

Founded by coach Renée Comeau, the Dieppe Fencing Academy opens its doors this spring at the Dieppe Sports Centre, offering programs for youth and adults in foil and épée.

"We've seen a real appetite for fencing in the greater Moncton area," said Comeau. "This is a great opportunity to grow the sport and give more kids access to one of Canada's most rewarding Olympic sports."

**Programs Offered**

- Beginner Youth (ages 8–14) — Monday and Wednesday evenings
- Adult Learn-to-Fence — Thursday evenings
- Competitive Stream — by assessment

For more information on the Dieppe Fencing Academy, visit their page in our [club directory](/clubs/).
