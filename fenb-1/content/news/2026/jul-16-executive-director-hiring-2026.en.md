Fencing-Escrime NB (FENB) is a non-profit organization led by a volunteer Board of Directors, incorporated as the provincial branch of the Canadian Fencing Federation. Our mandate is to promote, develop, regulate, encourage and improve the sport of fencing in New Brunswick, and to represent and coordinate the interests of New Brunswick fencers.

We are now hiring our next **Executive Director** — a part-time contract role responsible for executing the projects and tasks that meet FENB's goals, implementing the policies set by the Board of Directors, and providing leadership, coordination, direction and guidance across FENB's activities.

### Duties and responsibilities

- Conducting general administration, such as record keeping, updating memberships with provincial and national bodies, and overseeing FENB's day-to-day operations
- Supporting the Board of Directors and implementing Board policy and decisions
- Managing and implementing the FENB SafeSport Program, including sport dispute resolution
- Monitoring and overseeing FENB finances
- Coordinating FENB tournaments and other events
- Managing risk as it relates to the sport of fencing in New Brunswick
- Representing FENB to regulatory bodies, other agencies, community and civic organizations, donors, funders, supporters, and the general public
- Fundraising, including seeking out and applying for relevant grants
- Coordinating and managing volunteers
- Maintaining communication with key stakeholders

### Essential qualifications

- Strong organization and time management skills (workload can vary significantly week to week)
- Demonstrated effective leadership skills
- Self-motivated with a strong desire to succeed
- Ability to work independently, but capable of delegating tasks to others when needed
- Ability to work many weekends during the fencing season (September to June)
- Ability to travel in the province

### Asset qualifications

- Knowledge of and experience with Google Suite products and social media
- Experience working with a sport organization
- Knowledge of the sport of fencing
- Experience working with and directing volunteers
- Bilingualism (English and French) is an asset

This is a part-time contract position requiring the use of a home-based office in New Brunswick.

**To apply:** please send your resumé and cover letter to [fencingnb@gmail.com](mailto:fencingnb@gmail.com) by end of day **July 31, 2026**.
