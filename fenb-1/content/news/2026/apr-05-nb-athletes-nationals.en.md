---
title: "Three NB Athletes Selected for Canadian Championships"
date: 2026-04-05
category: announcement
summary: "Fencing New Brunswick is proud to announce that three athletes have qualified to represent the province at the 2026 Canadian Fencing Championships in Montreal."
---

Following the provincial selection event in March, Fencing New Brunswick is pleased to confirm the following athletes will represent New Brunswick at the 2026 Canadian Fencing Championships, held in Montreal from May 1–4.

**NB Team — Canadian Championships 2026**

- Madeleine Roy — Senior Women's Foil
- Thomas Arsenault — Senior Men's Épée
- Nicolas Bourque — Senior Men's Sabre

This is a historic year for the province, with athletes qualifying in all three weapons for the first time since 2018.

The athletes will travel with coach Pierre Fontaine. Fencing NB wishes the team the best of luck in Montreal.
