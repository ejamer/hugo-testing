Damocles Fencing Club is hosting the 2026 Summer Foil & Leadership Camp — a full week of technical foil training combined with leadership development for fencers ages 13–19.

**Camp Details**

- **Dates:** August 24–28, 2026
- **Location:** Damocles Fencing Club, Fredericton, NB
- **Ages:** 13–19
- **Hours:** 8:30 AM – 4:30 PM daily
- **Fee:** $325 per athlete (lunch and nutritious snacks included)

**What's Included**

- *Fencing Training & Tactics* — technical and tactical foil development, mental resilience and focus
- *Leadership Skills* — team building and communication, confidence and decision making
- *Experienced Coaching* — Joe Kinley & Isil Flynn, plus guest coaches and special workshops
