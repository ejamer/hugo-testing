Four New Brunswick fencers travelled to Rimouski, QC on June 13–14, 2026 for the Championnat provincial Junior, Senior, Vétéran, AAA (Québec Provincial Championships). NB athletes appeared in 3 events across Épée and Foil.

Congratulations to our podium finisher!

🥈 LAU Marcena — Senior Women's Foil

We also celebrate a strong top-16 finish from HAN Ethan (Junior Men's Épée, 7th).
