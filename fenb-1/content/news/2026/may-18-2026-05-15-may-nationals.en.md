title: "New Brunswick Fencers at the 2026 May Nationals"
date: 2026-05-18
category: "Results"
summary: "Thirty-six NB fencers competed across 31 events at the 2026 May Nationals in Quebec City, with Wendy Yano claiming two gold medals in veteran saber."
---

Thirty-six New Brunswick fencers travelled to Quebec City from May 15–18, 2026 for the Championnats nationaux de mai 2026 (May Nationals), one of the marquee events on the Canadian fencing calendar. NB athletes appeared in 31 of the 53 events across all three weapons and multiple age categories.

Congratulations to our podium finishers!

🥇 YANO Wendy — Veteran Women's Saber\
🥇 YANO Wendy — Vet-50 Women's Saber\
🥉 COLLINS David — Vet-40 Men's Épée\
🥉 FLYNN Isil — Vet-60 Women's Foil

We also celebrate strong top-16 finishes from STEVENS Flynn (Cadet Men's Saber), FLYNN Isil (Veteran Women's Foil), RICHARD Clara (Junior Women's Foil), STEVENS Ashelin (U-15 Women's Saber), COLLINS David (Veteran Men's Épée), ZHANG Zhirong (Junior Men's Épée), LAROCQUE Olivier (U-13 Men's Épée), RICHARD Clara (Senior Women's Foil).
