Provincial Championship Results — April 2026

April 20, 2026

Athletes from five clubs across New Brunswick gathered in Fredericton for the 2026 Provincial Fencing Championships. Competition was fierce across all three weapons — foil, épée, and sabre — with standout performances from junior and senior athletes alike.

Senior Results

  • Foil: First place to Madeleine Roy (Fredericton Fencing Club); second to James Leblanc (Moncton FC)
  • Épée: First place to Thomas Arsenault (Saint John Fencers); second to Sarah Kim (Fredericton FC)
  • Sabre: First place to Nicolas Bourque (Moncton FC); second to Emily Walsh (UNB Fencing)

Junior Results

Fourteen junior athletes competed across two age categories. Full results are posted on the Sport NB website.

Congratulations to all competitors, and thank you to the volunteers and officials who made this event possible.
