Registration Now Open: Junior Development Camp

March 15, 2026

Fencing New Brunswick is excited to announce that registration is now open for the 2026 Junior Development Camp, taking place June 6–7 at the Moncton Fencing Club.

Camp Details

  • Dates: June 6–7, 2026
  • Location: Moncton Fencing Club, Moncton NB
  • Ages: U17 and U20 athletes (born 2007–2011)
  • Weapons: Foil, épée, sabre — all three available
  • Cost: $95 per athlete (includes coaching, facility, and meals)

What to Expect

The camp features two full days of technical coaching, conditioning, and team bouting led by nationally certified coaches. Athletes will receive individual video feedback sessions and a personalized development plan.

How to Register

Registration is through Sport NB’s online portal. Spaces are limited to 24 athletes. Early registration is strongly encouraged.

Questions? Contact us at programs@fencingnb.ca.
